About Me

I am Tarekegn Dejen Mengistu (He/Him), a hydrologist and environmental data scientist specializing in physics-guided hydrologic framework, machine learning applications, and eXplainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) applied to large-scale hydroclimate data. I build uncertainty-aware models and generate data-driven research insights that improve decision-making under environmental variability.

Research Interests

  • Uncertainty Decomposition
  • River–Aquifer Interactions
  • Hydrologic & Water Quality Prediction
  • Physics-informed and Hybrid Hydrological Modeling
  • Probabilistic Learning for Environmental Systems
  • Uncertainty Quantification
  • Geospatial Analytics and Explainable AI (XAI)
  • Climate Variability, Extremes, and Water Resource Resilience
