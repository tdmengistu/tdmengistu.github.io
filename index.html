About Me

Tarekegn Dejen Mengistu|Tare (He/Him) is a Postdoctoral Researcher at Korea University working in AI-driven applications in water & environmental management. He earned his PhD from the University of Science & Technology (UST), where he concurrently held the position of UST Trainee Research Fellow at the Korea Institute of Civil Engineering and Building Technology. During his PhD, he developed integrated frameworks combining physics-based modeling, machine learning, and Bayesian deep learning to advance water management under environmental and anthropogenic pressures. His work aims to advance the understanding of hydroclimate and environmental variability to support robust decision-making.
