About Me

Tarekegn Mengistu (He/Him) is a researcher in hydrology and environmental data science. His work integrates physics-based hydrologic models with machine learning and eXplainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) to study large-scale hydroclimate and environmental systems. He develops uncertainty-aware models and data-driven research insights to advance understanding and support robust decision-making under environmental variability.
